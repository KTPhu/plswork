Birth and Death

  • Frank A. Mullikin was born on December 14th, 1842.
  • Frank A. Mullikin died on November 13th, 1908.
  • Died in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The cause of death is arteriosclerosis and stroke.

Education and Profession

  • Frank A. Mullikin was educated at the University of Pennsylvania, studying law and real estate.
  • He worked as a lawyer and conveyancer.
